# PatPatPat
## Introduction
PatPatPat allows players to pat a tamed cat or wolf.

# Feature
When patting:
- The entity that gets patted will nod, shake its tail, and sound with pleasure and ease, perhaps even slightly healed.
- The player may get experience.
